Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

How do payouts work for sellers?
We process payouts daily. Once your balance reaches $10, you can request a payout via PayPal, Venmo, or direct deposit. Payouts typically arrive within 24 hours.
Can I cancel my premium plan?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your premium benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are securely processed with 256-bit encryption.
How are notes priced?
Sellers set their own prices, but we provide guidance based on length, quality, and subject matter. Most notes range from $5-$20.
What's your refund policy?
Buyers can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase if they're not satisfied with their notes.
How do I become a premium seller?
After selling 10 notes with an average rating of 4.5+, you'll be eligible for our premium program. You can then upgrade in your account settings.
